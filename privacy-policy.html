Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Important: The QuickBooks Claude Desktop Integration operates entirely on your local device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or QuickBooks information to any external servers (except as required to communicate with Intuit's official QuickBooks API).

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the QuickBooks Claude Desktop Integration ("Application," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the Application, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Access

The Application accesses the following information from your QuickBooks Online account with your explicit authorization:

  • Customer information
  • Vendor information
  • Invoice data
  • Bill information
  • Account details
  • Transaction records
  • Financial reports
  • Other QuickBooks data you request through Claude Desktop

This data is accessed ONLY when you make a specific request through Claude Desktop and is processed locally on your device.

3. How We Handle Your Data

Local Processing Only

All QuickBooks data accessed through this Application is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to any third-party servers (except Intuit's QuickBooks API)
  • Not stored permanently by the Application
  • Not shared with any third parties
  • Not used for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose

Credentials Storage

Your QuickBooks authentication credentials (OAuth tokens) are:

  • Stored locally in a configuration file on your device (`.env` file)
  • Never transmitted to any server other than Intuit's official OAuth endpoints
  • Protected by your device's file system permissions
  • Under your complete control - you can delete them at any time

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT:

  • Collect or store your QuickBooks data on any external servers
  • Track your usage patterns or analytics
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Sell your personal information
  • Create user profiles or accounts
  • Monitor your conversations with Claude

5. Third-Party Services

The Application interacts with the following third-party services:

QuickBooks Online (Intuit)

The Application uses Intuit's official QuickBooks Online API to access your financial data. Your data transmission to and from QuickBooks is governed by:

  • Intuit's Privacy Policy: https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/
  • QuickBooks Terms of Service

Claude Desktop (Anthropic)

The Application operates through Claude Desktop using the Model Context Protocol (MCP). Data processing within Claude is subject to:

  • Anthropic's Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Claude's Terms of Service

Note: When using Claude Desktop, your conversations and data may be processed according to Anthropic's policies. Please review their privacy policy for details.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All communication with QuickBooks uses HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication protocol for secure access
  • Local storage of credentials protected by file system permissions
  • No permanent storage of QuickBooks financial data
  • Regular security updates to the Application

Your Responsibility: You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your device secure
  • Protecting your QuickBooks login credentials
  • Ensuring your `.env` configuration file is not shared
  • Regularly updating the Application

7. Data Retention

The Application does not retain your QuickBooks data. Data is:

  • Fetched on-demand when you make a request
  • Processed temporarily in memory
  • Cleared when you close Claude Desktop or end your session

OAuth refresh tokens are stored in your local `.env` file until you manually delete them or disconnect the integration.

8. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can access your QuickBooks data at any time through your QuickBooks account
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the Application's access to QuickBooks at any time through your Intuit account settings
  • Delete Credentials: You can delete your stored credentials by removing the `.env` file or uninstalling the Application
  • Stop Using: You can stop using the Application at any time without any data retention consequences

9. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used this Application, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

The Application operates through QuickBooks Online, which may store data in various locations. Please refer to Intuit's privacy policy for information about international data transfers and storage.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new policy on this page

Your continued use of the Application after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information

Note: We do not sell your personal information. Since all data processing is local, there is no personal information collected or stored by us that would be subject to these requests.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, these rights primarily apply to your relationship with Intuit (QuickBooks) and Anthropic (Claude).

14. Open Source and Transparency

This Application is built using open-source technologies and the Model Context Protocol. The code operates transparently on your local device, and you can inspect how it works at any time.
